How to Use

Getting Started

Once you've downloaded and installed the VatSys Plugin Installer, follow these steps:

  1. Launch the application from your desktop or start menu.
  2. Browse through available plugins.
  3. Click on "Download" to start installing your plugin.
  4. Once installed, restart VatSys to apply the plugin.
  5. When you want to update a plugin, simply click download again.
